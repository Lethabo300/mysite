About Us

  Pixie Mist is a classic fragrance which is more than just a scent-
  it's an invisible magic that transforms moments into memories.

  Founded in South Africa in 2025 by Lethabo Teffo, our boutique fragrance
   company draws inspiration from the mystical and whimsical world of pixies—
  those magical beings said to leave trails of captivating scents as 
  they dance through forests and gardens. Each Pixie Mist fragrance is 
  carefully crafted by our team of skilled perfumers who blend premium
  ingredients from around the world, with special attention to the unique
  floral treasures of our South African homeland.
 
  Discover the enchantment of Pixie Mist and let our magical fragrances, 
  born from the rich and diverse landscape of South Africa, transform your
   everyday moments into something extraordinary.
